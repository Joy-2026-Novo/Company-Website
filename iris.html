Product Description

The IRIS is a portable in-situ radiation identification system designed for isotope identification, quantification, efficiency calibration, and dose rate calculation. It is suitable for field measurements, emergency response, and regulatory inspections.

Applications

  • Environmental radiation monitoring
  • Nuclear facility inspections
  • Emergency response and security applications

Key Features

  • Isotope identification and quantification
  • Wide energy range gamma spectroscopy
  • Portable and battery operated
  • Visual and audible alarms
