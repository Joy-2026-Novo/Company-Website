<h2>EPR Dose Pellets</h2>
<p>
Alanine-based EPR dose pellets are used for precise measurement of absorbed radiation dose.
</p>

<h3>Applications</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Radiation processing</li>
  <li>Medical dosimetry</li>
  <li>Accident dosimetry</li>
</ul>

<h3>Key Features</h3>
<ul>
  <li>High stability</li>
  <li>Linear dose response</li>
  <li>Traceable calibration</li>
</ul>
