Product Description

Cesium Iodide (CsI) scintillation detectors are used for the detection and measurement of gamma and X-ray radiation. CsI crystals convert incident radiation into visible light, which is then detected using photomultiplier tubes (PMTs) or silicon photomultipliers (SiPMs).

Applications

  • Gamma-ray spectroscopy
  • Radiation monitoring and survey instruments
  • Nuclear physics research
  • Medical and industrial radiation measurements

Key Features

  • High light output
  • Good energy resolution
  • Available in CsI(Tl) and CsI(Na) variants
  • Compact and rugged detector assemblies

Detector Configuration

The detector assembly consists of an optically coupled CsI crystal, light sensor (PMT or SiPM), preamplifier, and signal processing electronics, optimized for stable and repeatable performance.
