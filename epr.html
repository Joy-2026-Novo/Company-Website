Product Description

EPR dose pellets are used for accurate measurement of absorbed radiation dose using Electron Paramagnetic Resonance (EPR) spectroscopy. The pellets are manufactured using high-purity alanine material to ensure stability, linearity, and reproducibility.

Applications

  • Radiation processing facilities
  • Medical and industrial dosimetry
  • Accident and retrospective dosimetry

Key Features

  • Excellent dose linearity
  • Long-term signal stability
  • Low energy dependence
  • Traceable calibration
