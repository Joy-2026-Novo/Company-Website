Product Description

NOVO-SCAN is a fully automated personnel contamination monitoring system designed for detection of beta and gamma radioactive contamination on personnel in controlled radiation areas.

Applications

  • Nuclear power plants
  • Radiopharmaceutical facilities
  • Research laboratories and controlled zones

Key Features

  • Simultaneous beta and gamma detection
  • Automated operation and alarm handling
  • High sensitivity plastic scintillator detectors
  • Industrial PC based control software
