Product Description

SPD-9300 is a semiconductor personal dosimeter designed for real-time radiation monitoring and dose management. It measures gamma and X-ray radiation exposure and supports ALARA radiation protection practices.

Applications

  • Nuclear power plants
  • Hospitals & medical radiation
  • Research laboratories and controlled zones
  • Industrial radiation safety

Key Features

  • Instantly displays radiation dose and dose rate
  • High sensitivity to X-ray and Gamma radiation
  • Supports radiation protection principle (ALARA)
  • Compact & Lightweight Design
