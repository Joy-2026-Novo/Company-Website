Semiconductor Personal Dosimeter – SPD-9300

SPD-9300

SPD-9300 is a semiconductor personal dosimeter designed for real-time radiation monitoring and dose management. It measures gamma and X-ray radiation exposure and supports ALARA radiation protection practices.

The SPD-9300 provides instant radiation dose display and systematic exposure management. It can be used as a standalone dosimeter or integrated into radiation monitoring systems in nuclear facilities and hospitals.

  • Nuclear facilities
  • Non-destructive testing (NDT)
  • Hospitals & medical radiation
  • Industrial radiation safety
  • Emergency & national defense

Radiological

  • Dose units: Sv, mSv, µSv
  • Dose rate: µSv/h, mSv/h
  • Energy: 30 keV – 6 MeV
  • Detector: Semiconductor

Electrical & Mechanical

  • Power: 3V Lithium cell (CR2450)
  • Battery life: >6 months
  • Weight: 55 g
  • Size: 80 × 48 × 20 mm

Environmental

  • Operating: −10°C to 50°C
  • Humidity: 40–90% RH
  • Drop: 1 m
  • EMI/EMC: IEC 61000
