Product Description

NOVO-SCINT is a compact digital photomultiplier base featuring an integrated high-voltage supply, preamplifier, ADC, and FPGA-based digital signal processing for spectroscopy applications.

Applications

  • Scintillation detector systems
  • Radiation spectroscopy
  • Portable and fixed radiation monitoring systems

Key Features

  • Integrated high-voltage supply
  • Digital signal processing with FPGA
  • Ethernet and USB-C communication
  • Compact and lightweight design
